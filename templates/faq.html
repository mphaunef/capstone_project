Using Spotify's API, Mulberry randomly chooses a song from within one of your randomly choosen playlists. With this information and your hand-picked genre choice, Mulberry then asks Spotify's API for a song recommendation. Mulberry's main goal is to expose users to new songs based on their previous tastes and favorite genres.
